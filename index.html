    Mission Statement

    We at CGS believe that all children deserve to excell in life. The sobering fact is that children today are less likely to acquire the knowledge they need to survive in today's world. Statistically, young adults cannot perform simple arithmatic without the aid of their phone.

    Learning how to think in a logical manner and collaborate with their peers affords children strong logic and interpersonal skill that is lacking in the 'text before talk' world. Code.org is the fun learning experience where children become the powerful person that they truly are!

    A meaningful donation to Code.org is the bedstone for securing the future of our children and the future of the the United States.
